Explore a Knowledge Graph and Social Network of Singapore Pioneers.

This is a proof-of-concept developed by National Library Digital Fellow Ng Siam Gek, in collaboration with Chris Khoo (Nanyang Technological University), for the project An Interactive Social Network of Singapore Pioneers.

The project objective was to explore the social networks of selected pioneers in post-war Singapore, using Singapore Infopedia, the National Library Boardâ€™s electronic encyclopedia on Singapore, to map the characteristics of pioneers including relationship types (e.g., kinship, business, industry, cultural, etc.) and entities (e.g., schools, organisations, activities, and places) that mediate these relationships.
